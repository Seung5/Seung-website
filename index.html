Neighborhood: Between Lincoln Center and Columbus Circle
Bounding Streets: 62,63rd Street, Columbus Ave, Broadway, Central Park West

- In this block, there are Lincoln Center, Central Park, and Columbus Circle in a walking distance which means it is a place where people can easily access to cultural, natural, commercial amenities around it.

- People can easily access to subway line 1,2,A,B,C,D within 500ft (66 St Lincoln Center, 59 St Columbus Circle). Also, there are 8 bus stops near the block including M5, M7, M10, M11, M20, M66, M104.

- Around the block, there are Education facilities (Fordham University, The Juilliard School, New York Institute of Technology, Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, The American Musical and Dramatic Academy), Religious facility (Church of St.Paul the Apostle, Manhattan LDS Temple), Medical facility (Mount Sinai West Roosevelt).

- Inside a block, the businesses have a pattern. The businesses near Columbus Ave are mostly restaurants, cafes and stores. Near the Broadway, which penetrates the block, there are banks, real estate agencies, travel agencies along with Lincoln Plaza Cinemas. Lastly, near Central Park West, most of the businesses are medical facilities.
